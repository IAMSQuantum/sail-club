The SAIL Club is for everyone, PIs, researchers, students and administration staff. SAIL aims to create a place where every month we can share what we have learned, ask questions and become inspired by some of the projects others in our institute are creating. Come along to find out more or get help with trying AI tools. You do not need to know how to code to use AI to build, chinese or english is enough to get started. Sign up to try out AI tools supported by the institute, ask for help to get started.

Come along and share (free) lunch with others from the institute who are interesting in using AI to make things better and easier.

If you have a project, tool or technique that you would be willing to talk about and share please contact the organizers.
